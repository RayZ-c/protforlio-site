## Implementation {.outline-only}


<details class="section-panel">
  <summary class="section-header">
    <span class="section-title">✮ Implementation & Optimization </span>
    <span class="section-icon">▾</span>
  </summary>

<div class="project-text">


**Volume Architecture:**
Different sound categories require different loudness profiles for immersion:

- **Punch SFX:** High clarity (players need immediate feedback)
- **Ability Audio:** Peaks above combat (signals important moments)
- **Voice Lines:** Prominent but not overwhelming (clarity for communication)
- **Background Ambient:** Subtle layer (adds atmosphere without distraction)

**Immersive Experience Design:**
- Layered sound design (multiple elements per action)
- Dynamic volume curves (sounds shift based on game state)
- Frequency separation (no competing sounds in same range)
- Spatial audio cues (directional punch sounds increase immersion)

**Community Feedback Integration:**
- Bug fixes addressing audio sync issues
- Volume adjustments based on player feedback
- Variation additions where players reported audio fatigue
- Compatibility updates for game patches

</div>

</details>
